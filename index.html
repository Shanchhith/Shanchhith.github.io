About me

I am a quantitative researcher working on causal programme evaluation, consumer behaviour, and structural approaches to demand estimation. My work spans health systems, packaged goods markets, and platform economics.

Currently based in Ahmedabad, I support a World Bank–funded Human Resources for Health project at IIM Ahmedabad's Centre for Management of Health Services, and work remotely with Stanford GSB on consumer behaviour pipelines.

I am targeting PhD programmes in Quantitative Marketing and Business Economics for Fall 2027, with specific interest in empirical IO, demand estimation, and health market design.
